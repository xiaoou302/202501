# Strida
### The Silent Gallery of Your Life

Strida is not just an interior design application; it is a companion for your journey in creating a home. We believe that every space holds the potential to resonate with the soul. Strida combines aesthetic inspiration with artificial intelligence to help you visualize and realize your ideal living environment. From curated design galleries to intelligent conversations about decor, and futuristic visualizations of your own room, Strida stands by your side, turning the house into a home where the heart truly belongs.

### Key Features

*   **Inspiration Gallery**
    Explore a curated collection of interior design aesthetics, from Minimalist to French Retro, finding the light and shadow that moves you.

*   **AI Discourse**
    Engage in deep conversations with our aesthetic consultant, exploring decoration trends, material choices, and design philosophies.

*   **AI Visualization**
    Upload a photo of your space and let our AI reimagine it in various styles, allowing you to preview the future of your home before lifting a finger.

*   **Renovation Records**
    Document every step of your transformation journey, preserving the memories of building your sanctuary.

# Strida (奥拉维)
### 生活的静谧画廊

Strida 不仅仅是一款室内设计应用，它是您构筑理想家园旅途中的伴侣。我们相信，每一个空间都潜藏着与灵魂共鸣的可能。Strida 将美学灵感与人工智能相结合，帮助您预见并实现理想的居住环境。从精心甄选的设计画廊，到关于装饰的深度智谈，再到您房间的未来式可视化，Strida 始终陪伴在侧，将房子变成心灵真正的归属地。

### 核心功能

*   **灵感社区**
    探索精心甄选的室内设计美学，从极简主义到法式复古，寻找那抹打动您的光影。

*   **智谈空间**
    与我们的美学顾问进行深度对话，探讨装饰趋势、材质选择与设计哲学。

*   **预见未来**
    上传您的空间照片，让 AI 以多种风格重新演绎，让您在动工前便能预见家的未来模样。

*   **装修档案**
    记录您改造旅程的每一步，珍藏构建心灵栖息地的点滴记忆。
