6. Introduction

9:43

Introduction

You are a reclusive alchemist, entrusted with your dying mentor's life's work: a cryptic "Journal of the Great Work."

It scrawls the 12 key steps to create the Philosopher's Stone, but the sequence is in chaos, the materials encrypted—"Red King," "White Queen," "Green Lion."

Your mentor failed countless times, but with his last breath, he prepared the laboratory. He believes you, his most gifted apprentice, can solve the final sequence.

Your task is twofold:

1. In The Study, safely decipher the journal and deduce the one true sequence.

2. When you are certain, enter The Laboratory and execute it, step by step, without error.
